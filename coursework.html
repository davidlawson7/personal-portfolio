The complete Space Rangers game. Downloads in the form of a .zip file. The file contains the games .exe along with with its_data folder. The C Sharp source files are also inside. While the game was a group project, I wrote all of C Sharp script myself.


The game is simple to setup and play. All you need to do is:


  1. Download Space Rangers via the link above.

  2. Connect your Kinect2 to your computer.

Physical Prototype & Presentation

We built a series of physical prototypes during the semester, which we used for testing before the Edge (which was also somewhat like a big test). One of these was an official piece of assessment, while the others were additional optional prototypes we built. The official physical prototype also came with a class presentation where we elaborated on our newest iteration of our concept. The actual testing was conduct on a range of individuals by either myself, Simon or Max.


Feedback on the classes concepts from the prototype presentations was formally submitted as a Appraisal Report. This work was done as a team and was a collection of all our opinions and thoughts.
